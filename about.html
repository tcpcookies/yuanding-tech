Who We Are

Precision Engineering Since 2018

Huizhou Yuanding Technology Co., Ltd. is a national high-tech enterprise headquartered in the Zhongkai Hi-Tech Zone of Huizhou, Guangdong — one of China's most dynamic technology and manufacturing hubs.

Founded in 2018, we specialize in the research, development, and manufacturing of brushless DC (BLDC) motors. Our core product lines include inner rotor BLDC motors, outer rotor hollow cup motors, brushless DC fans, precision gear motors, and marine propulsion systems.

With 9 technology patents and a dedicated R&D team, we serve clients across 20+ countries in industrial automation, medical equipment, marine technology, home appliances, and robotics sectors.

In 2024, we were officially recognized as a National High-Tech Enterprise by the Guangdong Provincial Government — a testament to our commitment to innovation and quality.

Our Journey

Milestones of Excellence

2018

Company Founded

Huizhou Yuanding Technology Co., Ltd. was established in the Zhongkai Hi-Tech Zone, Huizhou, with a focus on BLDC motor development and manufacturing.

2019

First-Gen Product Launch

Successfully launched the YD-IR series inner rotor BLDC motors and YD-BF brushless fans, establishing core product lines.

2020

International Expansion

Began exporting to overseas markets. Established partnerships with distributors in Europe, Southeast Asia, and the Middle East.

2022

Marine Motor Line Launch

Developed and launched the YD-MP series marine propulsion motors with patented waterproof technology, opening new market opportunities.
